## ðŸŽ¨ VISUAL DESIGN IMPROVEMENTS

### Color Psychology
- Keep bold **#E10600** red (urgency, appetite, energy)
- Add **warm accents** - Golden yellows (#F4C430) for premium feel
- Use **food photography** with vibrant colors
- **White space** - Let content breathe

### Typography Hierarchy
- Keep bold uppercase for headers (current style works!)
- Add **hierarchy** - H1 > H2 > H3 clearly distinct
- Readable body text - Increase line height to 1.6
- **Number emphasis** - Make prices, stats stand out

### Imagery Strategy
- **Hero image** - Overhead shot of both boxes, fully loaded
- **Box close-ups** - Show the beautiful layered pastry
- **Flavor photos** - Individual bureka shots, each flavor
- **Event photos** - Happy customers, catered events
- **Ingredient shots** - Fresh produce, homemade sauces
- **Behind-the-scenes** - Kitchen, baking process (trust)

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover effects** - Boxes lift slightly, shadows deepen
- **Selection feedback** - Satisfying animations when choosing flavors
- **Loading states** - Smooth transitions, never jarring
- **Error states** - Friendly, helpful (not scary red)
